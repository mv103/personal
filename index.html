Design CoMission Internship with LSW Architects

"The Marshall Park Project

As a project intern, I participated in the redesign of a local park with the objective of creating an ADA compliant and community uplifting environment. I worked in a mentor led team to research, coordinate, and deliver a final design presentation to clients and staff.

Our Objective

Currently in Marshall Park, there is a lack of inclusion for people of all abilities and ages. While Harper's Playground is taking a contemporary approach to Marshall's Park by breaking the stereotypical structure of a “playground,” the Marshall Park project aims to further communicate their mission of inclusivity throughout the park by incorporating diverse opportunities. We will give every visitor the opportunity to feed their mind, body and soul with fulfilling designs that improve community interaction, offer stimulating experiences with nature, and provide accessible athletic grounds. This will ensure that every visit to Marshall Park will encourage engagement with one's intellectual, emotional and physical vitality, regardless of limitations.
